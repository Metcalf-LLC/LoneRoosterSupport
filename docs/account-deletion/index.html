LoneRooster Support

Account deletion

You can delete your LoneRooster account directly from the app. Go to Profile > Account & Data > Delete Account.

What we delete

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your user profile and account settings
  • Your work schedule and preferences
  • Your notification settings and device tokens
  • Organizations you own and all their data, including:
    • All projects
    • Expenses and receipt images
    • Payments and income records
    • Voice memos and project photos
    • Time entries and check-ins
    • Change orders
    • Overhead expenses and expense categories
    • All uploaded files and images
  • Invite codes you created
  • Your check-in history in other organizations

What may be retained

If you are a member (not owner) of an organization, business records you created in that organization are preserved for the other members. This includes expenses, payments, time entries, memos, and photos. Your user ID remains on these records for historical accuracy, but you will no longer have access to the organization.

We may also retain limited records if required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. Backup copies may remain for a limited period before they expire from our systems.

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. If you own organizations with other members, they will lose access to all organization data when you delete your account.
