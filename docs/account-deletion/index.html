LoneRooster Support

Request account deletion

To request deletion of your LoneRooster account and associated data, email support@lonerooster.com from the email address tied to your account, or include that email in your message.

How to request deletion

  • Email support@lonerooster.com.
  • Use the subject line LoneRooster Account Deletion Request.
  • Include the email address connected to your LoneRooster account.
  • Include your organization name if your account belongs to a shared workspace.
Email support

What we delete

Once we verify the request, we will work to delete the account data associated with your LoneRooster profile, which may include account details, schedules, preferences, projects you solely control, uploaded receipts, payment photos, project photos, memos, and other related records stored for your use of the app.

What may be retained

If data is part of a shared organization, is needed to protect other collaborators, or must be retained for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance, we may keep limited records until the request is fully resolved. Backup copies may also remain for a limited period before they expire from our systems.

Questions about your account or deletion requests can be sent to support@lonerooster.com.
